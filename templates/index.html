About Us

At ForestGuard, we harness the power of Artificial Intelligence to predict forest fire occurrence and severity based on real-time weather conditions.

We use machine learning models trained on historical weather data based on Algeria to detect patterns that signal potential fire outbreaks.

Our system analyzes key indicators such as temperature, rainfall, wind speed, and dryness levels to provide accurate risk assessments.

In addition to fire prediction, we also keep users informed with the latest fire-related news and emergency contact information to ensure quick action in case of danger.
